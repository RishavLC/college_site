ABOUT HDC

Established in 2070 B.S., Himalaya Darshan College is a pioneering institution that offers innovative opportunities within a highly academic environment. Our objective is to provide value-based, quality education at the undergrad- uate level. We prioritize the personal and professional growth of our students, and achieve this through our experienced and distinguished faculty, as well as experts and professionals from across the nation. Our aim is to provide qualitative and competitive educa- tion to young students and to tackle the challenges of the present millennium. We offer a modern, values-based curriculum across five degree programs in three disciplines: Science, Management, and Humanities.

Our Mission & Vision

Learn about our commitment and future aspirations for our community.

Our Mission

The mission of Himalaya Darshan College is to empower students to become successful global citizens. We achieve this by providing students with both theoretical and practical exposure in all areas of study, thereby fostering academic and practical excellence. Our institution values students as valuable assets and creates an environment that enables them to meet industry standards. In addition to academic excellence, we also strive to instill in our students a mindset of critical thinking and a service-oriented attitude. Our ultimate objective is to encourage excellence, diversity, and engaged learning by providing a contemporary and accessible education.

Our Vision

Himalaya Darshan College offers a comprehensive educational experience that enhances both practical and theoretical abilities of students and provides an outstanding opportunity for making a positive impact on the world. It is a place where those who strive for excellence and constantly seek improvement can find ample opportunities to achieve their goals, which ultimately results in creating a better life not only for themselves but also for their community. At Himalaya Darshan College, we firmly believe in the potential of our students and are committed to providing them with the resources and support needed to realize their aspirations.
