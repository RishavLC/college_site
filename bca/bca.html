About BCA

The Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA) program is a semester based 4-years program offered by Faculties of Humanities and Social Science(FOHSS), Tribhuvan University(TU) . This aims to produce competent IT professionals who can work in different environments and handle various challenges in the technology field.
