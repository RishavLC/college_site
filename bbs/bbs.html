About BBS

BBS course is a four-year undergraduate degree in the field of management. It is a program based on yearly format. BBS program provides a strong foundation for anyone who wishes to gain knowledge and skills in the field of management. BBS curriculum is designed to equip students in various areas such as business, management, accounting, finance, marketing, and HR.
